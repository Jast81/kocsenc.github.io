Buying Guide

Guide for what?

Awesome idea popped in my head; to make buying things easier. Buying what? you may ask. Well I love building my own computers and working with hardware. Everytime I want to upgrade or build something, I dont want to be the foolish man and dump more money than I am intended to or I dont want to buy something crappy and be sad. As a result, I thought of building a tool to share my research when buying products. The products would involve Computer parts such as power supplies, RAM, Monitors, etc. Additionally, there would also be guides to buying Rubik's Cubes, Pyraminxi and timers. My thought for this is going to make people go through a questionare to make sure the products are what they see fit. The results would be based on loosley coupled products, so that the maintenance cost of updating wouldn't be overwhelming (i.e. you want a LCD 720p monitor rather than the ASUS 135XYAWERO1945.) I would of course, recommend what I bought and my favorites. :)
Expect to see this in the projects section soon!
